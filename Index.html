The Man & His Mission

Veteran. Diplomat. Visionary.वयोवृद्ध। राजनयिक। दूरदर्शी।

Colonel Mayank Chaubey is no ordinary commentator. He brings to the table what few can — genuine boots-on-ground military experience forged in the Indian Army, combined with elite diplomatic training as Director at the Sushma Swaraj Institute of Foreign Service (SSIFS), Ministry of External Affairs.

"Strategy without clarity is noise. I cut through the noise — that is RannNeeti."

— Colonel Mayank Chaubey, #MayankSays

His expertise spans national security doctrine, India's foreign policy architecture, wellness frameworks as Principal Consultant at the Yoga Certification Board (YCB), and frontier ventures as Vice President at Xenaverse. Today, as Advisor at RBSVM, he shapes institutional strategy at the highest level.

On X, his handle @col_chaubey has become a must-follow destination for unfiltered strategic insight — calling out hypocrisies, celebrating India's diplomatic rise, and building a community of strategic thinkers through #MayankSays and #RannNeeti.
